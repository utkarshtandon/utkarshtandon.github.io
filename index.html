Quantifying Parkinson's With Intelligent Analytics.

With our iOS app, OneRing creates a whole new way for phyisicans and Parkinson's patients to interact. Movement data collected over the span of a day is analyzed and generated reports are immediately sent to the physician. They can then alter their medication prescriptions to better suit their patient based on PD movement severities at every hour of the day.
